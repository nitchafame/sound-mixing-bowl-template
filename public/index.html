scar mixing

Everyday cuts and scrapes.

During this uncertain time, I often had nightmares, anxiety, and discontent. I dreamed about my family members, woke up felt like a crack in my chest. The worst part is I don’t remember a thing; will I forget? Will I be forgotten? This is like a bruise that I hadn’t have noticed before; when I do, it damages. The reoccurring feeling of the one’s far from home is expanding. The sense of missing pieces, the sore, and the pain lurking during the day caught me when I lost. It’s the 8th year after I moved here to NYC. Even I go back and forth between Bangkok and NYC, but where is actually my home. I miss the time we sit together.
