Will I be forgotten? Like a bruise that I hadn’t have noticed before; when I do, it quite damages. The reoccurring feeling of the one’s far from home has swelled. The sense of missing pieces, the sore, and the pain lurking during the day caught me when I fall. It’s been so long since I moved here. Where is it actually my home? I miss the time we sit together. How’s that sound like?
